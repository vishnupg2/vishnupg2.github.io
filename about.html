I am an AR/VR product designer with experience working on early zero-to-one initiatives, with strong visual craft, and a spatial design background. For the past 12 years I have developed a wide skillset in visual design, interaction design, motion graphics, 3D content creation, and 2D/3D prototyping.

At Meta Reality Labs, I designed the early voice experiences on Quest and AR wearables as well as UI foundations on glasses. Previously I was a UI/UX designer bringing joy to users with delightful and interactive features for Google Search. Before that I was designing 3D, AR, and VR projects as a freelancer and for top brands at an experiential agency. Reach out at vishnupg2[at]gmail.com.
